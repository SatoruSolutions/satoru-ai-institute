Artificial intelligence is changing far more than productivity. It is changing how humans think, learn, solve problems and make decisions.

Our Mission

Satoru AI Institute is an independent initiative dedicated to advancing AI-native thinking through practical frameworks, capability assessment and public insights.

Our goal is to help individuals and organisations develop the curiosity, communication skills and judgement required to work meaningfully with artificial intelligence.
