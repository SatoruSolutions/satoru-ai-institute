Welcome

Artificial Intelligence is changing far more than productivity. It is changing the way humans think, learn, solve problems and make decisions.

Satoru AI Institute is an independent initiative dedicated to advancing AI-native thinking through practical frameworks, capability assessment and public insights.

Our Mission

We believe the future belongs not simply to people who can use AI tools, but to people who can think effectively with AI.

Our goal is to help individuals and organisations develop the mindset, communication skills and judgement required to collaborate with artificial intelligence.
