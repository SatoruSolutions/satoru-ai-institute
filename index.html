Human Intelligence × AI Capability

Thinking better with AI.

Satoru AI Institute explores how human beings can think, learn, create, decide, and pursue greater independence with the support of AI.

The question is no longer whether AI is intelligent. The question is whether we know how to work intelligently with AI.

Why this institute exists

Many discussions about AI end too early. Some people conclude that AI is only a faster search tool, a writing assistant, or a machine that produces unreliable answers. Others focus only on automation, productivity, or replacing workers.

We believe the deeper question is how different forms of AI can extend one human being’s ability to understand, communicate, remember, experiment, and act. Used well, AI may help people overcome individual limitations and move closer to their dreams, independence, and freedom.

What we study

AI Capability

How effectively a person can work, think, learn, and create with AI.

Human–AI Collaboration

How people and AI systems can improve one another through dialogue, reflection, and practice.

Mind and Intelligence

What AI reveals about human reasoning, curiosity, judgement, memory, and self-awareness.

Open Discussion

Articles, responses, examples, and ideas managed by authors and supported by AI.

Not a course marketplace

The Institute is not currently designed to sell courses. Training and professional certification belong to a separate part of the Satoru ecosystem. This site begins with ideas, frameworks, articles, discussion, and the development of a common language for AI capability.
